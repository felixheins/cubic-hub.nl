Work Smart: Focus Your Energy on the Right Job Applications

Applying for jobs in the Netherlands as a foreign applicant can be challenging. Surveys indicate that foreign candidates often send out dozens of applications before securing an interview. Instead of exhausting yourself by spending hours on each application, adopt a strategic approach: minimize the energy spent on initial applications and maximize preparation for interviews.

Why You Should Work Smarter, Not Harder

Most job seekers receive responses from only a fraction of the applications they send. Instead of perfecting each submission, you should allocate your time wiselyâ€”focus on quantity at the initial stage and quality when it comes to interviews.

How to Apply Efficiently

  1. Create a solid base CV and cover letter: Use a general template and tweak only the key details.
  2. Apply in bulk: Set a daily or weekly goal for applications.
  3. Track responses: Monitor which companies respond to refine your strategy.
  4. Go all in on interviews: Invest time in researching the company and preparing thoroughly once you get an interview opportunity.
