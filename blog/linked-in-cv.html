How to Download a PDF Version of Your LinkedIn CV

LinkedIn provides an easy way to download a professionally formatted PDF version of your profile, which can serve as a CV. This document can be uploaded to Cubic Hub for further optimization and application enhancement.

Steps to Download Your LinkedIn CV as a PDF

  1. Log in to LinkedIn: Open LinkedIn and sign into your account.
  2. Go to Your Profile: Click on your profile picture or select "View Profile" from the menu.
  3. Download as PDF: Click the "More" button (next to the "Add profile section" button) and select "Save to PDF."
  4. Save the File: Your LinkedIn profile will be downloaded as a PDF file on your device.

Why Use Your LinkedIn PDF CV?

Your LinkedIn CV provides a concise and structured summary of your work experience, education, and skills. However, for better job applications, you should customize and optimize it further.
