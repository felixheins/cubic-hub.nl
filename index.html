Cubic Hub

Get a Job-Interview

With an AI optimized CV and Cover letter.

It is a job to get a job but AI can help. Cubic Hub analyses your CV and the job opening and provides you a custom CV and cover letter tailored for the job opening. Just tweak and send it. Cubic Hub maximizes the chances to pass the automated assessments of your application and puts you on top of the pile for HR to invite you to an inverview.

  • Try the first one for free

What it does

Format

Format the application to be perfectly readable by Automates Tracking Systems which are likely to read your application first.

Keywords

Similar is not good enough. Make sure the keywords in the job opening are in your application where suitable and score high in the first assessments.

More context

Introduce more context to the cover letter. Cubic Hub looks up the mission and project the employer is recruiting for. The cover letter references how your experience adds to their goals.

Apply a lot

Just upload your current CV and add the link to the job opening. You get to apply a lot so you can focus on the winners.
